Be gracious to us, O God, and bless us;* let your face shine upon us, and have mercy on us.• So will your ways be known upon earth* and all nations learn your saving help.• Let the peoples praise you, O God;* let all the peoples praise you.• Let the nations be glad and exult* for you rule the world with justice.• With fairness you rule the peoples,* you guide the nations on earth.• Let the peoples praise you, O God;* let all the peoples praise you.• The earth has yielded its fruit* for God, our God, has blessed us.• May God still give us his blessing* till the ends of the earth revere him.•
