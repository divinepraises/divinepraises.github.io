O God, make haste to my rescue,* Lord, come to my aid!• Let there be shame and confusion* on those who seek my life.• O let them turn back in confusion,* who delight in my harm,• let them retreat, covered with shame,* who jeer at my lot.• Let there be rejoicing and gladness* for all who seek you.• Let them say forever: “God is great,”* who love your saving help.• As for me, wretched and poor,* come to me, O God.• You are my rescuer, my help,* O Lord, do not delay.•
