On the holy mountain is his city*  cherished by the Lord.• The Lord prefers the gates of Zion*  to all Jacob's dwellings.•  Of you are told glorious things,*  O City of God.• Rahab and Babylon I shall count among those who know me;*  foreigners, Tyre, Ethiopia, these will be her children• and Zion shall be called "Mother"*  for a man was born there.• It is he, the Lord Most High,*  who founded her.• In his register of peoples the Lord writes:*  "These princes are her children,"• and while they dance they will sing:*  "In you all find their home."•
