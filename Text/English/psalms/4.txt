When I cried out to Him,* the God of my righteousness heard me.• You gave me relief in my distress:* have mercy on me and hear my prayer.• You sons of men, how long will you be hard-hearted? *Why do you love vanity and practice deceit?•  Know that the Lord works wonders for His faithful. *The Lord will hear me when I cry out to Him.•  Be passionate, but do not sin; * Repent in your bed for the words of your heart.• Offer a sacrifice of righteousness, * and hope in the Lord.•  Many are those who say, *“Who will show us good things?”• The light of Your countenance, O Lord, *has left its mark upon us.•  You have put gladness in my heart. *of their wheat, wine and oil.•  I will lie down in peace to sleep,* for You, O Lord, have let me dwell in hope.•
